Citrus and Garlic Prawns Recipe

Prawns grilled with tangy flavors of orange juice, lime juice, coriander and garlic.

Ingredients of Citrus and Garlic Prawns

  • 6-7 prawns
  • 1 tsp of coriander leaves, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chopped garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1+1/2 Tbsp of orange juice
  • 1 cup bread crumbs

Instructions

  1. Add to the prawn coriander, garlic, mustard, oil, lemon and mix well.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add the orange juice to give it a citrus-y flavor.
  4. Mix the prawns well. Now on a flat plate/surface spread some bread crumbs.
  5. Coat the prawns well with the bread crumbs which would give it a nice crisp exterior.
  6. Now skewer them on sticks.
  7. Heat about a teaspoon of olive oil in a flat pan. Cook for about 8-10 minutes.
  8. Decorate with some lime wedges and coriander.

Mini Mushroom Pizza Recipe

A delicious snack for parties, these Mini pizzas topped with mushrooms, corn and cheese are sure to get everyone's attention and cravings sorted.

Ingredients of Mini Mushroom Pizza

  • 2 Pizza base
  • 1 cup Cheese
  • 1 cup Mushrooms
  • 1 cup Sweet corn kernels
  • For Pizza Sauce:
  • 3 cups Tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp Onions
  • 1 tsp Garlic
  • 1 tsp Rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Take pizza base on a counter top. Use a cookie cutter to cut small circles and make dimples in it using a fork. Pop it in the preheated oven and leave for 5 minutes.
  2. In a pan, saute the garlic and onion. Add tomato puree, rosemary, salt. Cook till the sauce is nice and thick. A pinch of sugar can be added to sweeten the sauce a bit.
  3. Take toasted pizza bases, pour some sauce. Top it with mushrooms, sweet corn kernels and grated cheese.
  4. Bake in the oven for 2 minutes till the cheese melts.

Jeera Vegetables Recipe

A melange of sliced potatoes, carrots and spinach enhanced with the bright, peppery flavour of cumin powder.

Ingredients of Jeera Vegetables

  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • tsp jeera
  • 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic chilli paste
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 cup palak puree
  • 8-9 Baby potatoes
  • 5-6 Baby carrots
  • to taste salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan.
  2. Add jeera, onions, ginger garlic chilli paste, potatoes and carrots.
  3. Pour the palak puree and let it cook for sometime.
  4. Put some salt, pepper and coriander. Mix well.
  5. Put some butter and cream. Mix well and add lime juice.
  6. Garnish with some cream, red chilli powder and coriander. Serve hot.

Udon Noodles Salad Recipe

Vegetables and assorted dressing mixed in with udon noodles to create a scrumptious meal.

Ingredients of Udon Noodles Salad

  • 100 gms udon noodles
  • 1/2 medium raddish
  • 1/2 zucchini
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • 3 soaked shitake mushrooms
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1/2 medium spring onion or scallion
  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp vegetarian oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sushi vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Cook udon noodles in boiling water for 5 minutes.
  2. Drain out the water.
  3. Rest the noodles in a bowl.
  4. Use a spaghetti peeler to peel cucumber, yellow squash, medium size radish, carrot, a scallion and shitake mushrooms, half a red bell pepper finely chopped; add all the veggies to the bowl.
  5. Mix well
  6. For the vinaigrette (oriental inspired vinaigrette): a tablespoon of sushi vinegar, 1 teaspoon of ight soy sauce
  7. teaspoon of vegetarian oyster sauce, 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil, give it a mix.
  8. Season with salt to taste.
  9. Add the udon noodles to the veggies, and mix with the vinaigrette.
  10. A few sprigs of coriander.
  11. Mix with the veggies.

Machhi Amritsari Recipe

Fish fillets coated with a thick batter made of gram flour, yoghurt, eggs, tangy lemon juice and deep fried till golden.

Ingredients of Machhi Amritsari

  • 500 gm fish fillets/ fish fingers
  • 50 gm ginger garlic paste
  • 10 gm red chilli powder
  • 20 ml lemon juice
  • 05 gm ajwain
  • 200 gm gram flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 gm yoghurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Clean, wash and cut the fish into fillets or fingers.
  2. Apply salt, lime juice, ginger garlic paste and red chilli powder.
  3. Make a batter of gram flour, yoghurt, eggs, ajwain, salt and water.
  4. Marinate the fish in the batter for 10 minutes.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the fish until lightly brown and crisp.
  6. Sprinkled with chaat masala, lemon wedges and serve hot.
