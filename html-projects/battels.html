List of Famous Indian Historical Battles

  1. Name: Battle of Panipat
    Date: 1526
    Description: The first Battle of Panipat marked the beginning of Mughal rule in India, as Babur defeated the Delhi Sultanate.

  2. Name: Battle of Plassey
    Date: 1757
    Description: The Battle of Plassey was a pivotal moment in British India's history, where the British East India Company's victory led to their control over Bengal.

  3. Name: Battle of Haldighati
    Date: 1576
    Description: The Battle of Haldighati was fought between the forces of Maharana Pratap Singh and the Mughal Emperor Akbar, resulting in a stalemate.

  4. Name: Battle of Buxar
    Date: 1764
    Description: The Battle of Buxar was fought between the British East India Company and a coalition of Indian rulers, leading to British dominance in eastern India.

  5. Name: Battle of Plassey
    Date: 1857
    Description: The Indian Rebellion of 1857, also known as the Sepoy Mutiny, challenged British rule and marked a significant turning point in India's struggle for independence.
