Landmarks

  1. Name: Clock Tower
    location:Dehradun
    Description:Clock tower, also known as "ghanta ghar" is a very famous tourist attraction. It was started in the year 1948 and got completed in 1953.

  2. Name:India Gate
    location:New Delhi
    Description:The India Gate is a war memorial located near the Kartavya path on the eastern edge of the "ceremonial axis" of New Delhi, formerly called Rajpath.

  3. Name:ISKON Temple
    location:New Delhi
    Description:Sri Sri Radha Parthasarathi Mandir, generally known as the ISKCON Delhi temple, is a well known Vaishnav temple of Lord Krishna and goddess Radha in the form of Radha Parthasarathi.

  4. Name:Tirupati Balaji
    location:Andhra Pradesh
    Description:Situated in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, Tirupati is known for Lord Venkateshwara Temple, one of the most visited pilgrimage centers in the world.

  5. Name:Chittorgarh Fort
    location:Rajasthan
    Description: India's largest fort spans nearly 700 acres, Covering around 691.9 acres in all, there are a total of 65 structures within the fort. It looks like a fish when viewed aerially with the circumference being 13 kms
